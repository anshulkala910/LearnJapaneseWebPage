When entering a store, be quiet and remember to put your phone on silent. When waiting in line for the counter, be patient and polite to others. When it is your turn, go up to counter and say "ko-ni-chi-wa" (Hello). If you want to ask how much a certain item is, say "i-ku-ra-de-su-ka".
