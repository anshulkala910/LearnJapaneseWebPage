In the Writing section, you will learn the three writing styles in Japanese: Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji. In the Hiragana and Katakana pages, you will match the Hiragana/Katakana characters to their corresponding letters. To match a pair, drag the English letter to its corresponding Japanese letter and drop it. To aid in your understanding, you can be able to play the sound of the characters in Japanese.
In the Kanji pages, you will encounter some important and most frequently seen Kanji in Japan and their meanings. These Kanjis will be shown to you in Hiragana/Katakana character and English letters. Also, the words will be discussed in the "Common Words" section, where you can know the meaning and pronunciation of it.
