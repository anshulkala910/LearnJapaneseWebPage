When entering a train in Japan, always stand on the left or right side of the door of the train. This is to make it easier for people in the train to get off. Please be patient and let everyone off the train. Also, when in the train, make sure your phone is kept silent and you don't talk on the phone. If you are listening to music, make sure it is not disturbing other people.
